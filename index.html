What is "Gaga 411"?

As I've said, this application contains many facts and information about the world-wide pop-star, and Queen of Pop herself, Lady Gaga! When viewing this app, you can read some quick facts about her, including her birthday, height, eye color, REAL name, and much more. You'll also be able to read her full story from getting thrown into dumpsters to dropping out of college to pursue fame.

You're also able to view her albums as well as listen to the songs on each album. And if that's not enough, I even provide music videos and photos from each era: The Fame, The Fame Monster, Born This Way, and ARTPOP!

So just sit back, relax, and prepare to feast your mind on everything Gaga with Gaga 411.
